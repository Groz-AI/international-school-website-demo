Academics

Three pathways tailored to each learner’s stage—Early Years, Primary, and Secondary—with a balanced focus on knowledge, skills, and character.

Early Years

Play-based, inquiry-led learning that builds curiosity, early literacy and numeracy, and social-emotional skills. Children explore through stories, role-play, outdoor learning, and carefully structured routines that nurture independence and confidence.

  • Safe, nurturing learning environments
  • Phonics and early numeracy foundations
  • Learning through exploration and storytelling

Primary

Deepening literacy and numeracy, project-based learning, and strong character education. Students collaborate on cross-curricular projects, apply problem-solving strategies, and develop communication and digital skills.

  • Inquiry projects and cross-curricular links
  • Formative assessment and targeted support
  • Clubs, sports, arts to broaden interests

Secondary

Challenging pathways with guidance towards top university placements and future-ready competencies. Students personalize programs, pursue advanced subjects, and benefit from university counseling, internships, and leadership opportunities.

  • Advanced subject choices and guidance
  • University counseling and careers support
  • Service learning, leadership, and internships
